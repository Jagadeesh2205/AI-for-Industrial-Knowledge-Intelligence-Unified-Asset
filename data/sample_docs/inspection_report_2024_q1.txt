2. INSPECTION FINDINGS
================================================================

2.1 Pump P-101 (Centrifugal Pump, Grundfos CR 95-3)
    Inspection Type: Visual + Ultrasonic Thickness
    
    Findings:
    a) Discharge piping (6" CS, ASTM A106 Gr B):
       - Minimum thickness: 5.2mm (nominal: 7.11mm)
       - Corrosion rate: 0.15mm/year
       - Remaining life: 7.5 years (acceptable)
    
    b) Suction piping bend:
       - Thickness: 5.8mm (nominal: 7.11mm)
       - Erosion pattern noted on outer radius
       - Corrosion rate: 0.20mm/year
       - Remaining life: 5.5 years (monitor)
    
    c) Foundation bolts:
       - 2 of 4 foundation bolts show minor surface corrosion
       - Recommendation: Wire brush and apply protective coating
    
    d) Pump casing:
       - No visible cracks or damage
       - Paint deterioration noted — repaint recommended
    
    Status: ACCEPTABLE with monitoring

2.2 Heat Exchanger HX-301 (Shell & Tube)
    Inspection Type: Eddy Current + UT
    
    Findings:
    a) Tube bundle: 3 tubes plugged (of 248 total) — within 2% limit
    b) Shell thickness: 11.5mm (nominal: 12.7mm) — acceptable
    c) Tube sheet: No defects detected
    d) Gasket: Minor weeping at channel end — replace at next turnaround
    
    Status: ACCEPTABLE

2.3 Compressor C-201 (Reciprocating, Dresser-Rand)
    Inspection Type: Visual + Vibration Analysis
    
    Findings:
    a) Vibration levels within acceptable range (3.2 mm/s)
    b) Suction valve: Minor pitting observed — monitor
    c) Discharge valve: Good condition
    d) Piston rod runout: 0.02mm (limit: 0.05mm) — acceptable
    e) Bearing temperature: 58°C (limit: 75°C) — normal
    
    Status: ACCEPTABLE

    NOTE: Similar bearing failure to P-101 incident (IR-2022-047) 
    occurred on C-201 in April 2022 when non-OEM bearings were used.
    Since switching to OEM bearings, no issues reported.

2.4 Vessel V-201 (Pressure Vessel, Carbon Steel)
    Inspection Type: UT Thickness + MPI
    
    Findings:
    a) Shell thickness: 18.2mm (nominal: 20mm, retirement: 14mm)
    b) Nozzle N1 (inlet): Corrosion under insulation (CUI) detected
       - Thickness reduced to 8.5mm (nominal: 11mm)
       - CRITICAL: Repair required before next quarter
    c) Internal inspection: Minor pitting on bottom head
    
    Status: CRITICAL — CUI repair required
