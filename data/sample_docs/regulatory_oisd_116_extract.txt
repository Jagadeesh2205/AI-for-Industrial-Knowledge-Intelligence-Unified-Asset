SECTION 4: INSPECTION AND MAINTENANCE
================================================================

4.1 General Requirements
All fire protection systems and safety-critical equipment shall be
inspected and maintained as per the schedules specified herein.
Records of all inspections shall be maintained for a minimum of 10 years.

4.2 Inspection of Static Equipment (Pressure Vessels, Storage Tanks)
4.2.1 External visual inspection: Quarterly
4.2.2 Ultrasonic thickness measurement: Annually
4.2.3 Internal inspection: Every 3 years or at shutdown, whichever is earlier
4.2.4 Corrosion Under Insulation (CUI) inspection:
      - All insulated vessels operating between 50°C and 175°C: Annual CUI inspection
      - Vessels with known CUI history: Quarterly monitoring
      - Method: UT from external, or insulation removal at suspect areas
4.2.5 Fitness for Service assessment required when:
      - Wall thickness reaches 80% of retirement thickness
      - Any single reading below minimum required thickness
      - Corrosion rate exceeds 0.25mm/year

4.3 Inspection of Rotating Equipment
4.3.1 Vibration monitoring: Monthly for critical, quarterly for general
4.3.2 Bearing temperature monitoring: Daily (manual or continuous)
4.3.3 Oil/lubricant analysis: Quarterly
4.3.4 Performance monitoring (flow, pressure, current): Continuous
4.3.5 Alignment check: After every maintenance intervention

4.4 Documentation Requirements
4.4.1 Inspection records shall include:
      - Equipment tag number and description
      - Date of inspection and name of inspector
      - Type of inspection performed
      - Findings with measurements
      - Comparison with previous readings
      - Recommendations and due dates
4.4.2 Non-compliance findings shall be reported to:
      - Plant Manager within 24 hours (Critical)
      - Plant Manager within 7 days (Major)
      - Documented in next quarterly report (Minor)

SECTION 5: FIRE PROTECTION SYSTEMS
================================================================

5.1 Fire Water System
5.1.1 Fire water pumps shall be tested weekly
5.1.2 Fire water network pressure test: Annually
5.1.3 Fire hydrants: Semi-annual inspection and flow test
5.1.4 Minimum fire water storage: 4 hours at maximum demand rate

5.2 Fire Detection and Alarm
5.2.1 Smoke/heat detectors: Monthly functional test
5.2.2 Manual call points: Quarterly test
5.2.3 Alarm panel: Daily check of status
5.2.4 Complete loop test: Annually

5.3 Fixed Fire Suppression
5.3.1 Foam systems: Annual foam compound testing
5.3.2 Sprinkler systems: Quarterly valve inspection
5.3.3 Gas suppression systems: Annual cylinder weighing

================================================================
SECTION 7: SAFETY DISTANCES
================================================================

7.1 Minimum distances between equipment:
- Pump house to process unit: 15 meters
- Flare to process unit: 90 meters (based on radiation study)
- Storage tank to process unit: 30 meters
- Control room to process unit: 30 meters

================================================================
SECTION 9: EMERGENCY RESPONSE
================================================================

9.1 Emergency response plan shall be reviewed annually
9.2 Mock drills: Minimum 2 per year (1 announced, 1 unannounced)
9.3 Mutual aid agreements with neighboring facilities required
9.4 Emergency communication system shall have battery backup for 4 hours
