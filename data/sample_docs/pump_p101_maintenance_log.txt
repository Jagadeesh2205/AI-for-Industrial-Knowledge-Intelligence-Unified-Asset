Work Order: WO# 2023-0892
Date: 18/07/2023
Type: Corrective Maintenance
Assigned To: Suresh Patel (Senior Technician)

Description:
Pump P-101 reported minor leak at discharge flange.
Operator noticed small drip during routine rounds.

Work Performed:
1. Isolated pump per LOTO procedure SP-045
2. Depressurized and drained pump
3. Inspected discharge flange gasket — found gasket degradation
4. Replaced gasket with spiral wound gasket (SS316/Graphite)
5. Re-torqued bolts to 85 Nm per OEM specification
6. Hydro-tested at 6.3 bar (1.5x operating pressure)
7. Returned to service, no leak observed

Parts Used:
- Spiral Wound Gasket DN80 (SS316/Graphite) x 1

Total Downtime: 4 hours
Status: COMPLETED
